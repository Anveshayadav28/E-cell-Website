Demystifying Entrepreneurship

At E-Cell, we aim to inspire university students to start their own ventures and dispel the many fears that surround starting one's own company.

About Us

E-Cell SSTC is a student run non-profit organization which has been established in Shri Shankaracharya Group of Institutions, Bhilai, since 07/10/2017. E-Cell, SSTC targets to help in the development of entrepreneurial ecosystem by enabling easy and efficient interaction between its major components spanning students, working professionals, aspiring and existing entrepreneurs, mentors, angel investors, venture capital firms and corporate through events like interactive sessions, competitions and activities.

VISION

To develop the entrepreneurial environment by providing easy and effective interaction of the student body, experienced professionals, successful entrepreneurs, investors, venture capitalists and corporate.

MISSION

We look towards incorporating the entrepreneurship and intrapreneurship as potential abilities of the individuals by being a platform to cater the same.

    Flagship Events

    AD-MAD SHOW

    The E-cell at Shri Shankaracharya Technical Campus, Bhilai, hosted the "Ad-Mad Show," an event where students showcased their advertising skills. Participants developed and presented advertisements, demonstrating their understanding of marketing strategies and creativity. This event provided a professional platform for students to display their talents and learn about the significance of advertising in business.

    IDEATHON

    The E-cell at Shri Shankaracharya Technical Campus, Bhilai, orchestrates the "Ideathon," a cerebral arena where visionaries converge to pitch groundbreaking ideas. This event is a crucible for innovation, offering a stage for entrepreneurial minds to unveil potential ventures and engage with seasoned mentors, fostering a culture of inventive excellence and strategic business insights within the academic community.

    100+ ideas

    500+ participants

    One-Dollar Venture

    The E-cell at Shri Shankaracharya Technical Campus, Bhilai, proudly hosted the "One-Dollar Venture," an innovative entrepreneurial challenge. Participants were tasked with transforming a modest investment of one dollar into a profitable venture, showcasing their business acumen and strategic prowess within a dynamic, competitive environment. This event exemplified the spirit of entrepreneurship and ingenuity among students, fostering a culture of creative problem-solving and economic savvy.
