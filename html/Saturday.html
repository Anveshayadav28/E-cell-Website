3-D PRINTING

Join us for an immersive 3D printing session where we will delve into the fascinating world of additive manufacturing. In this session, participants will learn the intricacies of 3D printing, from understanding the technology behind it to practical hands-on experience in creating 3D models. Explore the possibilities of turning digital designs into physical objects, and gain valuable insights into the various materials and techniques used in 3D printing. Whether you're a novice or have some experience in the field, this session offers a comprehensive overview of 3D printing that will equip you with the knowledge and skills to bring your ideas to life in three dimensions. Don't miss this opportunity to unlock the potential of 3D printing and expand your horizons in the realm of modern manufacturing.

MAKING OF DRONE

Step into the realm of drone technology with our exclusive session on the making of drones. Join us as we delve into the intricacies of drone construction, from understanding the components to assembling your very own drone from scratch. In this session, participants will gain hands-on experience in soldering, programming flight controllers, and configuring drone systems. Explore the principles of aerodynamics and flight mechanics as we guide you through the step-by-step process of building a fully functional drone. Whether you're a beginner or have some background in drone technology, this session offers a comprehensive overview that will empower you to create your own aerial devices. Elevate your skills and knowledge in drone-making and embark on a thrilling journey into the world of unmanned aerial vehicles.
