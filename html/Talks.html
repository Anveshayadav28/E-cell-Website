TALKS AND WORKSHOPS





Talk Show

Becoming a successful entrepreneur is not easy, only a few make through it.

E-Cell SSTC organised a talk show with one of the youngest entrepreneurs of India, a TEDx speaker, an influencer Mr. Farrhad Acidwalla on 13 September 2021 who shared his knowledge and experience with students and E-Cell members.

WEBINAR ON "DESIGN THINKING AND ITS APPLICATIONS"

E-Cell SSTC organized a workshop on "Design Thinking and its applications" on 24th and 25th of August 2021.

Innovation and design thinking is for anyone willing to change their mindset, not only about challenges and problem solving, but also about creating something new. We believe great innovators and leaders need to be great design thinkers. Holding on to this faith that design thinking is a catalyst for innovation and bringing new things into the world.

WEBINAR ON "HOW MARKETING HELPS IN GROWING YOUR VENTURE"

E-Cell SSTC organized a workshop on "How Marketing helps in growing your Venture" on 08 August 2020. The objective of the workshop was to give a brief introduction about how good marketing makes the company look smart and great marketing makes the customer feel smart. Students and E-Cell members enthusiastically participated in the workshop. The feedback received from the members was overwhelming.

WEBINAR ON "FUTURE OPPORTUNITIES OF STUDENTS DURING & BEYOND LOCKDOWN"

E-Cell SSTC organized a workshop on "Future opportunities of students during & beyond Lockdown" on 02 July 2020 in collaboration with Neardy Academy . The objective of the workshop was to give a brief introduction about the future opportunities for students during and beyond Lockdown.

Students and E-Cell members enthusiastically participated in the workshop .It was a complete hands-on session of 2 hours duration. The feedback received from the members was overwhelming.
